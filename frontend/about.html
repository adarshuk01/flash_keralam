Our Mission

The NEWS* was founded on a simple belief: that access to accurate, impartial, and deeply reported journalism is a cornerstone of a healthy democracy.

We cover global affairs, technology, business, culture, health, and sports — not to push an agenda, but to give our readers the full picture so they can form their own well-informed views.

Every story we publish goes through a rigorous editorial review. We correct mistakes openly, name our sources wherever possible, and maintain a strict firewall between our advertising and editorial teams.

Our Values

  • 01

    Independence

    Our editorial decisions are made entirely by our journalists — never by advertisers, governments, or interest groups.

  • 02

    Accuracy

    We verify before we publish. Every claim is fact-checked against primary sources, and we issue corrections prominently.

  • 03

    Transparency

    We are open about our funding, methods, and any potential conflicts of interest in all our reporting.

  • 04

    Accountability

    We hold the powerful to account while treating all people — regardless of their prominence — with dignity and fairness.

Advertising & Independence Disclosure

The NEWS* is supported in part by advertising revenue, including ads served by Google AdSense and other third-party ad networks. These advertisements help fund our journalism and keep our content free to readers worldwide.

Advertisers have no influence over our editorial content. All story selection, sourcing, and reporting decisions rest solely with our editorial team. Sponsored content, when it appears, is clearly labelled as such and is separate from our independent reporting.
